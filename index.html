Engineering Journey

My father is who initially inspired me to one day become an engineer. In my childhood, I can recall countless hours helping my father as he fixed issues with our family car. This fascination was instilled in me from hours spent with my father, assisting him as he worked on our family car's engine. His love for mechanics inspired me to pursue a similar path.

Over time, I developed the aspiration of pursuing Aerospace. Staring up at planes flying by during my workouts on the track fueled my rooted passion for mechanics, developing this aspiration. I envision becoming a mechanical engineer, specializing in Aerospace. To me it's not just a goal, but a lifelong dream.
